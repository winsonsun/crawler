# Cognitive Instincts & Archetypal Patterns
*The structural library of trans-domain wisdom used to drive autonomous strategy.*

---

## 🟢 Archetype: The Immune System Response
**Origin Domain:** Immunology / Biology
**Pattern Recognition:** 
*   Sudden, site-wide "inflammation" (Turnstile/CAPTCHA) triggered by specific behavior.
*   "Antibodies" (IP bans) targeting the most active "Pathogens" (fast crawlers).
**The Wisdom:** High-speed interaction is a pathogen. Symbiosis requires looking like "Natural Flora" of the site.
**Instinctive Action:** 
*   Shift to **Symbiotic Posture**: Introduce "Noise Actions" (clicking non-critical links, scrolling, lingering) that simulate a "Healthy Visitor" to evade the immune sensors.

---

## 🟢 Archetype: The Tectonic Shift
**Origin Domain:** Geology / Seismology
**Pattern Recognition:** 
*   Small "tremors" (broken CSS on minor pages).
*   Structural "fault lines" where old API endpoints are being buried by new JavaScript-heavy overlays.
**The Wisdom:** A minor breakage is rarely isolated; it is a sign that the entire "Plate" (the site's architecture) is moving.
**Instinctive Action:** 
*   **Pre-emptive Hardening**: When a tremor is detected, automatically switch all extractors to **Vision-First** (Omni-Solver) rather than relying on DOM selectors, anticipating the total collapse of the old structure.

---

## 🟢 Archetype: The Labyrinthine Trap
**Origin Domain:** Architecture / Mythology
**Pattern Recognition:** 
*   Circular redirects or "infinite" detail pages that lead back to the home page.
*   "Honeypot" links that are hidden from humans but visible to DOM-scanners.
**The Wisdom:** The most direct path is designed for automated prey. The "Secret Path" is often obscured or non-linear.
**Instinctive Action:** 
*   **Shadow Navigation**: Follow the "Breadcrumb" trail or the "Related Content" links instead of the primary index. Prioritize metadata found in the `<head>` or via "hidden" visual cues over the main UI buttons.

---

## 🟢 Archetype: Metabolic Entropy
**Origin Domain:** Thermodynamics / Physiology
**Pattern Recognition:** 
*   High token usage ("sweating") with low data yield.
*   "Basement Bloat" (scripts, logs, and patches accumulating in the root).
**The Wisdom:** Complexity is a heat-loss. A heavy organism is slow and fragile.
**Instinctive Action:** 
*   **Forced State Compression**: Automatically trigger the `manifold-compressor` to "freeze" messy patches into clean, hierarchical library functions, restoring "Metabolic Efficiency."

# ✨ The Spark Sandbox (The Incubation Chamber)
*A space for unvalidated analogies, playful 'fun,' and curious patterns. Empirical Validation is NOT required here.*

## ✨ Spark: The 1920s Jazz Club (The Speakeasy)
**Concept:** Some sites (like JavBus) hide their "Real" content (Magnets) behind a "Secret Knock" (the `existmag=all` cookie).
**Curiosity:** Does the site behave differently if we act like a "Regular" (someone with a long-lived session) versus a "Tourist" (a fresh browser)?
**Trans-domain Leap:** Instead of "bypassing," we should "socialize" with the site to earn access.

## ✨ Spark: The Mycelial Network
**Concept:** Site adapters shouldn't be "Stiff" monoliths; they should be like fungi, sending out "Hyphae" (micro-requests) to feel the environment before committing to a full crawl.
**Curiosity:** Can we detect a "Tectonic Shift" by sending a single `HEAD` request to a random asset file instead of the homepage?
**Trans-domain Leap:** Intelligence should be decentralized and "felt" at the edges.
