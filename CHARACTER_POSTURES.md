### 🔄 The Swap Mechanism
To swap a character, the Sentinel must:
1.  Update the **"Current Active Posture"** in this file.
2.  Recalibrate the `EVOLUTIONARY_RETROSPECTIVE.md` to use the corresponding Persona's voice.
3.  Filter the `COGNITIVE_INSTINCTS.md` to prioritize the character's primary archetypes.
