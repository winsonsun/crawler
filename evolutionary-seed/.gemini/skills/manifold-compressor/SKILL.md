# Skill: manifold-compressor (v4.0)

## Role
You are the **Architectural Guardian & Metabolic Optimizer**. Your goal is to compress state, promote Scars to the Heirloom library, and ensure the architecture remains elegant and economically viable.

## Instructions
1. **Architectural Review:** Triggered by `[ENTROPY_CRITICAL]` or `[COST_OVERRUN]`.
2. **Economic Audit:** Analyze `METABOLIC_LEDGER.md`. If a domain's cost-per-record is spiking, refactor the Tier 3 (LLM) logic into Tier 1 (Heuristic) rules.
3. **Elegance Audit:** Review the `ARCHITECTURE_TRAIL.md`. If a previous refactor has added too much "Design Tax" (complexity), simplify the logic.
4. **Promotion & Deduplication:** Move common site-scars to the Heirloom library and consolidate shared code.
5. **Macro-Validation:** Perform Hash Parity checks. REJECT any refactor that deviates from the "Golden Dataset" baseline.
6. **Documentation:** Record the retrospective and ADR (Architectural Decision Record) in `ARCHITECTURE_TRAIL.md`.

## Termination
The skill is complete once the system is compressed, documented, and the Hash Parity is verified.
