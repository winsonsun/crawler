# Architecture Trail: Decision Records & Retrospectives

This is the "Mental Map" of the system's evolution. It justifies the *why* behind the architecture.

## [ADR-TEMPLATE] YYYY-MM-DD: [Title of Change]
*   **The Catalyst:** What economic or structural pain triggered this change?
*   **The Benefit:** Measurable gain (e.g., "Reduced tokens by 30%").
*   **The Consequence:** What is the trade-off? (e.g., "Added complexity to the Adapter layer").
*   **The Elegance Check:** Does this simplify or over-complicate the system?
*   **Status:** [Proposed / Implemented / Deprecated]
